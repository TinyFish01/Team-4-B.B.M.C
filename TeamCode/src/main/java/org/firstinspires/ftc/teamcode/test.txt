Mecanum Control Equations

Movement:

FORWARD AND BACKWARDS:

double y = -gamepad1.left_stick_y;

leftMotor.setPower(y);
rightMotor.setPower(y);

Explanation: If Left stick is pushed forwards, both motors are given a POSITIVE value, causing the bot to move forward.
             If the Left Stick is pushed backwards, both motors are given a NEGATIVE value, causing the bot to move backwards.

Remember, Y stick is reversed

ROTATION:

double y = -gamepad1.left_stick_y;
double rx = gamepad1.right_stick_x;

leftMotor.setPower(y + rx);
rightMotor.setPower(y - rx);

Explanation: Subtracting the power from one motor and adding it to the other will cause the robot to turn
             Similar to the forwards and backward movement the rotation involves giving the motors positive and negative feeds
             If the right stick is pushed to the right, the left wheels are given a positive value, causing them to move in a forward direction.
             The right wheels are given a negative value, causing them to move in the other direction, causing the robot to turn right.
